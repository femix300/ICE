## Summary
Implements the manual replay endpoint for failed webhooks. It takes a dead-lettered webhook ID, validates it, and re-adds it to the BullMQ webhook delivery queue.

## Scope
- `src/controllers/webhook-deliveries.controller.ts`: Validates `req.body` using Zod and enforces `AppError` on bad inputs or missing deliveries.
- `src/repositories/webhook-deliveries.repo.ts`: Extended to fetch delivery payloads by ID.
- `tests/unit/webhook-deliveries.controller.test.ts`: Added full `vitest` unit tests covering Zod validation failure, 404s, and successful queue addition.

## Test Plan
- Unit test coverage added for controller edge cases.
- Re-queuing payload verified visually.

## Risk
Low. Validates payload before pushing to queue.
