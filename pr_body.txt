## Summary
Adds dead-letter handling (DLQ) for webhook deliveries that fail after 5 retry attempts, protecting against infinite loops for system DLQ alerts. Also adds a `POST /v1/webhook-deliveries/:id/replay` endpoint to manually re-queue failed jobs with reset attempts.

## Approach
- Modified `webhook-deliveries.repo.ts` to add `markDeadLetter` and `byId`.
- Added loop prevention checks to `webhook-delivery.worker.ts` so `system.webhook_dead_letter` events do not trigger cascading DLQs.
- Stubbed the `webhook-deliveries.controller.ts` and `webhook-deliveries.routes.ts` ready for integration once the core Express setup merges.
